The road so far..

If I start with telling you all about me, we could be here all day. So instead, I thought I would start telling you how I learned Web Design.

At the beginning of 2025, I decided I wanted to pursue the idea of publishing a book that I wanted to write called “Dizzy Dimension”. So, I started the process of enrolling in school and was recommended to take the course that had a fancy title
“e-business Development Engineering” , but after a few months I just started using the simple name which be “Web Design”. What I didnt realize at first is that taking the course would be teaching me more then one new language. From XML to HTML and lots more…

The best part about starting the process of coding the website is when things start coming to life and its a lot more complex than you would think.

One thing I would like to explain is that there are a couple different ways to design a website. You can use frameworks like “square space” or “Wix” and with those you can just pick items from list that would look good together and the framework with just do the background work. Or you could use Programs like “VS Code” or “WebStorm” and you can do all the coding from behind the scenes putting everything into place step-by-step until everything comes together and thats what formed the whole website your currently reading…
